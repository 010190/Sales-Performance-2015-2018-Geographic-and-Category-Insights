## Insights
- **Geographic:** West and East regions dominate sales; New York City and Los Angeles are top-performing cities
- **Product:** Phones and Chairs are the best-selling sub-categories
- **Customer:** Consumer segment represents over 50% of total sales
- **Growth:** Consistent year-over-year sales increase throughout the period
